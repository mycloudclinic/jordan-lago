About

Founded in 2014, LO2S emerged from Beirut's thriving rave scene with a vision to push the boundaries of events worldwide. Now a Dubai-based practice, we produce nothing short of spectacular moments that shift beliefs, activate the senses and inspire awe.


Our expertise spans dimensional experiences and technical innovation, bending light, sound and motion into elevated sensory experiences and narratives. What-the-fuck moments that challenge perception. Massive stage productions and intimate brand environments.

Process

Attack

We strike hard and fast, decoding your vision and amplifying its potential. Raw concepts become engineered possibilities. From initial consultation to rapid prototyping, we define technical requirements, spatial constraints, and creative direction – setting the foundation for what's possible.

Sustain

This is where energy levels peak and hold. A period of relentless build and refinement. We test boundaries, break conventions, and fine-tune every detail – from intricate lighting sequences to precise kinetic movements. The momentum never drops.

Release

The moment of impact. We deliver with surgical precision, transforming spaces through meticulous programming and operation. When pressure peaks, we thrive. Our team handles everything from technical setup and real-time visual integration to live operation and show control, ensuring flawless execution every time. Pure bliss.
